Top three activities to do in Washington, D.C.

The Smithsonian Institution

The world-famous Smithsonian with its many museums! One of the most popular is the Air and Space Museum.

The Kennedy Center

Where to go to see the hottest shows in town! You can see many plays and concerts here.

The Sports Scene

Washington is home to the Capitals (NHL), Nationals (MLB), Commanders (NFL), and D.C. United (MLS).
